Given the pokemon data is not available to the client
When the pokemon have yet to become available to the client
Then
- the page should render the text "loading...".
- the loading text should have a role of "status".
- the first generation of pokemon should be requested

Given the pokemon data is not available to the client
When the data becomes available to the client
or
Given the pokemon data is available to the client
When the page is viewed
Then
- This list should be a series of cards (chunks of information), one for each pokemon.
- Each cards should feature a pokemon name, id, types, and default sprite.
- The name should have a role of "heading".
- The id should have a role of "paragraph".
- The types should be encapsulated by ordered list with a role of "list".
- The types should each have a role of "listitem".
